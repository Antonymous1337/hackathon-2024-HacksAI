Deepfake Defender


To use, hover your mouse over the image you would like to scan, then press "S".

A green "R" symbol represents 80% confidence or more that the image is Real.

A red "F" icon represents a less than 80% confidence that it's real, meaning a high chance the image is Fake.



Disclaimer: Deepfake detection isn't perfect. Even in the event of a passing image, please be cautious trusting everything you see on the internet.
